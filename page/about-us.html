What is Bchan Forum ?

Bchan Forum is a Japanese Anime and Culture lovers community forum.
Here you can ask, share, ask for help and recommend a Japanese anime.

What should i do if i want to make a Discussion on Bchan ?

If you want to make a discussion about Anime, you must have a Bchan ID (Bchan Forum Account) and you can create one on the list page!
